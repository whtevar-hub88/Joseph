Career

Joseph Morgan's first ever acting audition was for Harry Potter
and the Chamber of Secrets for Tom Riddle.
Joseph Morgan has starred in Hex, The Line of Beauty,
Doc Martin, Casualty, Mansfield Park, and Ben Hur.
He has also appeared in supporting roles in films and TV series.
Joseph Morgan starred in The Vampire Diaries, Immortals,
The Originals, and Brave New World, and was ranked number
84 on TV's Sexiest Men of 2011.
